Goldmine Agent

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Goldmine Agent is a content workflow tool for the Goldmine Trading education brand. This policy explains what information is used when a user connects a TikTok account and publishes educational video content through the app.

Information We Use

Goldmine Agent may use TikTok OAuth information authorized by the user, including a basic account identifier, authorization tokens, and posting permissions required for the selected workflow. The app also processes the video file, caption, and publishing settings chosen by the user.

How Information Is Used

  • To confirm the TikTok account connected through Login Kit.
  • To upload an approved video as a TikTok draft when the user selects upload.
  • To publish an approved video when the user selects direct publishing.
  • To display connection status and prevent posting to the wrong account.

Storage and Retention

OAuth tokens are used only for the connected posting workflow. Local development versions may store tokens on the user's device for testing. Users can revoke access through TikTok account settings or request removal by contacting us.

Third-Party Platforms

Goldmine Agent connects to TikTok using TikTok developer APIs. Use of TikTok is also governed by TikTok's own terms, privacy policy, and platform rules.

Financial Content Disclaimer

Goldmine Agent supports educational trading content operations. It does not provide investment advice, execute trades, manage funds, or guarantee trading results.

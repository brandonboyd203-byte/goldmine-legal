Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Goldmine Agent ("the Service") is committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We store OAuth tokens for connected social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube) locally on your device. We do not transmit or store personal data on external servers.

2. How We Use Information

Tokens are used solely to post content to your connected social media accounts on your behalf.

3. Third-Party Platforms

By connecting your accounts you also agree to the respective terms and privacy policies of TikTok, Instagram/Meta, and YouTube/Google.

4. Data Retention

Tokens are stored locally and can be removed at any time by deleting the ~/.goldmine-agent/tokens.json file.
